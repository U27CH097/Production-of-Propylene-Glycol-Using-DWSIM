# Production-of-Propylene-Glycol-Using-DWSIM
This project involve Material Balance, Energy Balance, Cost Estimation and Simulation.
## Software Used : DWSIM


Engineered a closed-loop plant configuration in DWSIM for glycerol-to-propylene glycol conversion, successfully integrating a high-pressure gas recycle loop and a heavy organic recycle stream.
Achieved an exceptional overall plant yield of $99.1\%$ and optimized reactor conversion by continuously recycling unreacted intermediates back into the system.
Decreased fresh hydrogen consumption by $2.511 \text{ mol/s}$, achieving a permanent $1.66\%$ reduction in raw gas utility costs across a projected 8,400-hour operational year.
Recovered $1.23\text{ mol/s}$ ($0.113\text{ kg/s}$) of unreacted glycerol from vacuum distillation bottoms (MSTR-14) via a heavy recycle loop (RCY-01), driving chemical waste down to near-zero.
Stabilized final product purity at an exceptionally concentrated $97.96\text{ mol}\%$ 1,2-propylene glycol while eliminating once-through organic disposal dependencies.
